Queen's Hotel Kandy

Located in the heart of Kandy, Queen's Hotel is one of the oldest and most iconic hotels in Sri Lanka. With its colonial architecture and rich history, the hotel offers a unique blend of old-world charm and modern comfort.

The hotel features spacious rooms, a beautiful garden, and a restaurant serving delicious local and international cuisine. Its proximity to the Temple of the Tooth Relic and Kandy Lake makes it a perfect choice for travelers exploring the cultural capital of Sri Lanka.
