One Galle Face Mall

One Galle Face is Colombo's premier shopping and lifestyle destination, offering a world-class retail and entertainment experience. Located in the heart of Colombo, this mall features over 200 stores, including international brands, luxury boutiques, and local favorites.

The mall also boasts a state-of-the-art cinema, a wide range of dining options, and a stunning view of the Indian Ocean. Whether you're shopping, dining, or simply enjoying the ambiance, One Galle Face is a must-visit destination in Sri Lanka.

Colombo City Center

Colombo City Center (CCC) is a modern shopping and lifestyle hub located in the bustling heart of Colombo. With over 200 retail outlets, a food court, and a rooftop dining area, CCC offers a unique shopping and dining experience.

The mall is also home to a state-of-the-art cinema and a fitness center, making it a one-stop destination for entertainment and wellness. Its sleek design and vibrant atmosphere make it a favorite among locals and tourists alike.

Kandy City Center

Kandy City Center is the largest shopping mall in the hill capital of Sri Lanka, offering a mix of retail, dining, and entertainment options. Located in the heart of Kandy, this mall is a popular destination for both locals and tourists.

The mall features a variety of stores, from fashion and electronics to local handicrafts. It also includes a food court, a supermarket, and a cinema, making it a one-stop destination for all your needs.
